High-Assurance Security Architecture

Compliance QuickStart LLC is a security architecture and advisory firm dedicated to the principle that effective security is universal: identify the requirements, design the architecture, and enforce the controls.

We specialize in bringing the rigor of failure-intolerant engineering to broader enterprise challenges. Drawing on deep experience in nuclear propulsion, semiconductor manufacturing, and defense systems, our methodology translates complex regulatory frameworks into verifiable, engineering-centric solutions.

We reject the "checklist" approach to compliance. Instead, we treat GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) as an architectural constraint, ensuring that systems are secure by design regardless of the specific regulatory overlay.
